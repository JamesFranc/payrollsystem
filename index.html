J Jacks Payroll System

CS323 Term Project

This is the Alpha Release of J Jacks Payroll Software.

Capabilities of the program include:

  • Employee Clock In/Out
  • Employee Management
  • Timesheet Management
  • Cheque Printing (Simulated)
  • Printing Tax Forms (Simulated)
